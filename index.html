About Me.

I'm a student at the University of Michigan studying Computer Science Engineering with a passion for photography.

I grew up in Northern Michigan and have always been fascinated by the beauty of the outdoors. Photography really started to grab my attention when I realized I could capture my own perspective on that beauty. That expanded and I now do all types of photography from landscape to sports to portrait.

I currently live in Ann Arbor but I love to traveling all over and seeing new places.
